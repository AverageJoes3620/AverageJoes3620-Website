FIRST Mission Statement

The mission of FIRST is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting Mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.

Our Team

We are Team 3620 the Average Joes from Saint Joseph High School. We officially became a team in 2011. Since then, we’ve been actively raising awareness of the importance of STEM education in our school district and our community through FIRST Robotics. Our team has one simple mission: to inspire our youth and to explore opportunities in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We believe STEM education is something any “Average Joe” can be a part of.
